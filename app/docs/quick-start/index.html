Using Origin

Quick start

Looking to quickly add Origin to your project? Download the origin__typography.css and place it in your project's css folder. Copy-paste the stylesheet <link> into your <head> after all other stylesheets to load the CSS.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="file:///yourproject/css/origin__typography.css">

Don't forget to update the path to match your folder structure. And that’s it—you’re on your way to a beautiful Origin typography in your projects.
